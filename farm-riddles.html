Farm Machinery Riddles

1. What has four wheels and flies? Answer: A farm truck!

2. I go up and down but never move. What am I? Answer: A tractor!

3. What has teeth but cannot bite? Answer: A rake!

4. I have a body and wheels but I do not have a soul. What am I? Answer: A combine!

5. I’m full of holes but I can hold water. What am I? Answer: A net (for fishing)!
